Play Asteroids

Instructions

Navigate you space ship around the game world using by arrow keys or by 'W', 'A', 'S', 'D'. Stay aware of asteroids (red), they can damage your ship and decrease your lives. Moreover destroy asteroids as many as possible using your Space Bar key. By destroying asteroids you will gain a score. The largest asteroid needs to be hit three times, middle sized asteroid two times, small asteroid just once. If asteroid is not a small one, it will break up into several smaller pieces if being hit enough.
